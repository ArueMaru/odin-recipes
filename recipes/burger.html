How to Make a BURGER

A burger is one of the greatest foods in all of existence. It can be prepared in many ways, contain various foods, and be eaten in any number of ways. Here is what you need to know to make a basic BURGER.

Ingredients

  • Bread (Buns)
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Mayo
  • Burger Patties (cooked)
  • Onions
  • Tomato
  • Lettuce
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Mix Ketchup, Mustard, and Mayo together to create "burger sauce"
  2. Place top bread (bun) next to the bottom bread
  3. Slather some of the previously made burger sauce onto the insides of the top and bottom breads (buns)
  4. Put lettuce on top of the sauce of the bottom bread (bun)
  5. Place two cut tomato slices on the lettuce
  6. Set two cut rings of onions on top of the lettuce
  7. Lay down burger patties on the onions
  8. Insert cheese between patties
  9. Place the top bun upon the ingredients you combined
  10. Congratulations! You made a BURGER.
